<p><p>The electronic drum kits market features three primary types of drumheads: rubber, Mylar, and mesh or silicone. Rubber heads are durable and affordable, providing a firm feel but limited realism. Mylar heads offer a more acoustic-like response and are often used in higher-end kits for their sensitivity. Mesh or silicone heads are prized for their quiet operation and realistic rebound, making them ideal for practice and recording. Each type caters to different preferences and playing styles.</p></p>
