<p><ul><li>Natural Rubber</li><li>Synthetic Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber gym flooring is categorized into two primary types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber flooring is made from latex extracted from rubber trees, offering excellent elasticity, durability, and slip resistance, making it suitable for various fitness applications. Conversely, synthetic rubber flooring is composed of man-made materials, such as EPDM or SBR, providing versatility in design and coloration. Synthetic options often feature enhanced shock absorption and lower maintenance, appealing to a broader range of commercial and residential users in fitness environments.</p></p>
